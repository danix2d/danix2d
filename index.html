HOW I DO IT

LEARN AND DON'T GIVE UP

Creating games is a great journey, learning from your failures and celebrating your successes. There will be difficulties, a lot of them will put you in hard situations, but that makes the ride more memorable, rewarding and enjoyable. Don't give up on your dreams and never stop learning!

HAVING FUN

It's fun expressing my ideas to me and my friends as something you can see and touch. Watching my friends and family play the games I make, always amuses me.

TONS OF PASSION

If you want to be the best you need to love what you do. That's why I love creating my game ideas, it makes me happy making the games that I enjoy.
