HOW I DO IT

ABOUT ME

My name is Danny and I am young self-taught independent game developer. I do everything in the development process of my games, like the coding, game-art, drawing 2D or modeling 3D assets, level-design, debugging and testing etc.

SOFTWARE

To make my games, I use Unity a 3D cross-platform game development engine and C# programming language. I make my 2D assets with Krita, which is a free, open source painting program. For 3D assets I use Blender, which is also free, but very powerful 3D modeling software.

HAVING FUN

It's fun expressing my ideas to me and my friends as something you can see and touch. Watching my friends play the games I make, always amuses me. Get my games from Google Play and I hope you enjoy them too.
